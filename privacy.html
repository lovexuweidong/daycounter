Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to DayCounter.pro ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. DayCounter.pro is designed with privacy in mind:

  • All calculations are performed locally in your browser
  • We do not store your dates, events, or any personal data
  • We do not require account registration
  • We do not use cookies for tracking purposes

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Google AdSense

We display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Google Analytics

We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous data. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Cookies

We use cookies solely to remember your cookie consent preference. We do not use cookies to track your browsing behavior or store personal information. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data, there is minimal risk of data breaches. Your dates and calculations remain on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is safe for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or adults. Parents can feel confident that their children's dates and calculations are never stored or transmitted.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and similar regulations, you have the right to:

  • Access any data we hold about you (we hold none)
  • Request deletion of your data (no data to delete)
  • Opt out of personalized advertising via Google AdSense
  • Disable cookies in your browser

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@daycounter.pro
Website: www.daycounter.pro

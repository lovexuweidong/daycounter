About DayCounter.pro

DayCounter.pro is a free, simple, and beautiful tool for tracking your special days. Whether you're counting down to a birthday, anniversary, or vacation, or counting up from a memorable date, we've got you covered.

Our Mission

We believe that every day counts. Our mission is to help people around the world celebrate their special moments and track their personal milestones with ease and joy.

What We Offer

⏰ Countdown Tracker

Count down to birthdays, weddings, vacations, holidays, and any special event.

📅 Days Since Calculator

See how many days since you started dating, quit smoking, began a new job, or any milestone.

🔢 Date Calculator

Calculate the difference between any two dates in days, weeks, months, or years.

🎂 Age Calculator

Find your exact age in years, months, and days down to the day.

Privacy First

Your privacy matters. All calculations happen right in your browser—we never store, transmit, or see your dates. Your special moments stay yours.

Free Forever

DayCounter.pro is completely free to use. We're supported by advertising, which allows us to keep this service available to everyone around the world.

🚀 Getting Started

How do I count days until my birthday?

▼

Enter your birthday date in the Countdown section and click "Calculate". The tool will show exactly how many days until your next birthday. You can also add a calendar reminder!

How do I calculate days since an event?

▼

Go to the "Days Since" section, enter the event name (like "Started Dating") and the start date, then click "Calculate". The tool will show how many days have passed since that date.

Can I use different event types?

▼

Yes! We offer 7 preset event types: Birthday 🎂, Anniversary 💍, Wedding 👰, Vacation ✈️, Holiday 🎄, Exam 📚, and Custom ⭐. You can create unlimited custom event types!

📅 Calendar & Export

Can I export events to my calendar?

▼

Yes! Click the "Export to Calendar" button after calculating. This will download an .ics file that you can import into:

  • Google Calendar
  • Apple Calendar (iPhone/Mac)
  • Microsoft Outlook
  • Any calendar app that supports ICS

How do I add calendar reminders?

▼

Enable the "🔔 Enable Calendar Reminder" option before saving. You can choose when to be reminded:

  • On the day
  • 1 day before
  • 3 days before
  • 1 week before
  • 2 weeks before
  • 1 month before

How do I import the ICS file?

▼

Google Calendar: Settings → Import → Select the .ics file → Add

Apple Calendar: File → Import → Select the .ics file

Outlook: File → Open & Export → Import/Export → Import an iCalendar file

🔒 Privacy & Security

Is DayCounter free to use?

▼

Yes! DayCounter.pro is completely free to use. No registration is required, no hidden fees, no premium version. Just open the website and start using it!

Is my data private?

▼

Yes, 100%! All calculations happen in your browser - we don't store any of your dates or events on our servers. Your events are saved only in your browser's local storage (localStorage), which means only YOU can see them.

Do you use cookies?

▼

We use only essential cookies to remember your language preference and cookie consent. We don't track your browsing behavior or collect personal information.

⚙️ Technical

Does DayCounter work on mobile phones?

▼

Yes! DayCounter.pro is fully responsive and works perfectly on all mobile devices, tablets, and desktop computers. You can also add it to your phone's home screen like an app!

Do I need an internet connection?

▼

Yes, you need an internet connection to load the website. However, once loaded, your saved events work offline since they're stored in your browser.

Can I save events for later?

▼

Yes! Click "Calculate & Save" to store events in your browser. Your saved events will appear in the "My Saved Events" section. You can export them all at once or delete individual events.

What languages are supported?

▼

We currently support 4 languages: English 🇺🇸, Chinese 🇨🇳, Spanish 🇪🇸, and Japanese 🇯🇵. More languages coming soon!

🌳 Secret Tree Hole

What is the Secret Tree Hole?

▼

The Secret Tree Hole is a private space where you can write down your thoughts, feelings, secrets, or anything you want. Think of it as a digital diary that's completely private and stored only on your device.

Can anyone else see my Tree Hole entries?

▼

No! Your Tree Hole entries are stored 100% locally on your device using localStorage. No one else can see them - not even us. They're completely private.
